Privacy Policy for Spendia

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Spendia ("we," "our," or "us") is a personal expense tracking application developed by Kevin Campos Alfaro. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email Address: Collected during account registration for authentication purposes
  • Account Data: User profile information including subscription status and account creation date
  • Financial Data: Expense and income records you manually enter or create through voice/photo features
  • Custom Categories: Personalized spending categories you create

2.2 Subscription Information

  • Premium Status: Whether you have premium access (all launch users receive free premium access)
  • Subscription Type: Launch user, monthly, annual, or lifetime subscription status
  • Payment Data: When premium features become available, transaction information processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system

2.3 Temporary Processing Data

  • Audio Recordings: When using voice expense entry, audio is temporarily processed and immediately deleted after transcription
  • Receipt Photos: When scanning receipts, photos are temporarily processed and immediately deleted after data extraction

2.4 Device Information

  • Language Preference: Device language settings to provide localized experience
  • Local Storage: App preferences and cached data stored locally on your device

2.5 Bug Reports & Issue Tracking

  • Issue Reports: When you report an issue through the app, we collect your description of the problem, steps to reproduce (optional), and which screen/feature was affected
  • Device Information: Automatically collected technical information including device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro), iOS version, and app version to help diagnose issues
  • User Information: Your email address and display name to follow up on reported issues
  • Report Metadata: Date and time of report submission, issue status updates, and developer notes
  • Frequency Limit: Users can submit one issue report per week to ensure quality support

Privacy Note: Issue reports are private and only visible to you and the development team. They are never shared with other users or third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the Spendia service
  • User authentication and account management
  • Managing subscription status and premium features access
  • Processing voice and photo inputs using artificial intelligence
  • Generating expense analytics and summaries
  • Sending push notifications (with your permission)
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Troubleshooting and fixing reported issues
  • Communicating with users about their reported issues

4. Data Storage

4.1 Cloud Storage (Firestore)

The following data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore with encryption:

  • Email address
  • Account creation date
  • Premium status (isPremium: true/false)
  • Subscription details (type, status, granted by)
  • Expense and income records
  • Custom categories
  • Issue reports (title, description, steps, screen, device info, status, timestamps)

4.2 Launch User Benefits

All users who sign up during the launch period receive:

  • Free premium access to all features
  • Status marked as "launch_user" in our system
  • Continued free access even when premium subscriptions are introduced

5. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

5.1 Firebase (Google)

We use Google Firebase for:

  • Authentication: Secure user login and account management
  • Cloud Firestore: Encrypted storage of your user data, expenses, income records, and issue reports
  • Cloud Functions: Server-side processing of AI features and issue report notifications

Firebase Privacy Policy

5.2 OpenAI

We use OpenAI's API for:

  • Voice-to-text transcription and expense data extraction
  • Receipt photo analysis using GPT-4 Vision
  • Intelligent expense categorization

Important: Audio recordings and receipt photos are sent to OpenAI for processing and are NOT stored by OpenAI or Spendia after processing completes.

OpenAI Privacy Policy

5.3 Apple In-App Purchases

When premium subscriptions are introduced, payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all payment data.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with Firebase
  • API keys stored securely in cloud infrastructure (not in app code)
  • User authentication via Firebase Auth
  • Local device encryption for cached data
  • Access controls ensuring issue reports are only visible to the reporter and development team

7. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Subscription Information: Retained for the lifetime of your account
  • Audio Recordings: Deleted immediately after transcription
  • Receipt Photos: Deleted immediately after data extraction
  • Cached Data: Limited to last 6 months, older data automatically removed
  • Issue Reports: Retained for the lifetime of your account to maintain a history of technical support. Deleted when you delete your account.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data (including issue reports)
  • Export your expense data
  • Opt-out of push notifications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • View your subscription status and history
  • View all your submitted issue reports and their status

9. Children's Privacy

Spendia is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Costa Rica, including the United States (where Firebase and OpenAI servers are located). These transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards.

11. Future Premium Features

When premium subscriptions are introduced:

  • Launch users will maintain free premium access
  • New users will have the option to subscribe for premium features
  • Subscription management will be handled through Apple's App Store
  • This Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect any new data collection related to subscriptions

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open Spendia app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

All data including subscription information and issue reports will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
