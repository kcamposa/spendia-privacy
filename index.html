Privacy Policy for Spendia

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Spendia ("we," "our," or "us") is a personal expense tracking application developed by Kevin Campos Alfaro. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email Address: Collected during account registration for authentication purposes
  • Account Data: User profile information including subscription status and account creation date
  • Financial Data: Expense and income records you manually enter or create through voice/photo features
  • Budget Data: Monthly spending limits, budget categories, alert thresholds, and income tracking you configure
  • Custom Categories: Personalized spending categories you create

2.2 Apple Pay Transaction Data

  • Transaction Amount: The amount spent via Apple Pay (when you enable the optional iOS Shortcuts automation)
  • Merchant Name: The name of the merchant where the transaction occurred
  • Transaction Date: When the Apple Pay transaction took place

Important: Apple Pay integration is completely optional and requires you to manually set up an iOS Shortcut. We only receive this data if you choose to configure the automation. We do not have access to your Apple Pay account, payment methods, or full transaction history.

2.3 Subscription Information

  • Premium Status: Whether you have premium access (all launch users receive free premium access)
  • Subscription Type: Launch user, monthly, annual, or lifetime subscription status
  • Payment Data: When premium features become available, transaction information processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system

2.4 Temporary Processing Data

  • Audio Recordings: When using voice expense entry, audio is temporarily processed and immediately deleted after transcription
  • Receipt Photos: When scanning receipts, photos are temporarily processed and immediately deleted after data extraction

2.5 AI Usage Tracking

  • Daily AI Request Count: Number of AI-powered features used per day (voice entry, photo scanning, Apple Pay categorization)
  • Usage Date: Date of AI feature usage to enforce daily limits
  • Purpose: To manage service costs and enforce usage limits (Free: 20 requests/day, Premium: 100 requests/day)

Privacy Note: We track the COUNT of requests, not the content of your expenses or what you said/photographed.

2.6 Device Information

  • Language Preference: Device language settings to provide localized experience
  • Local Storage: App preferences and cached data stored locally on your device
  • App Check Token: Anonymous device verification token to prevent unauthorized access (does not identify you personally)

2.7 Bug Reports & Issue Tracking

  • Issue Reports: When you report an issue through the app, we collect your description of the problem, steps to reproduce (optional), and which screen/feature was affected
  • Device Information: Automatically collected technical information including device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro), iOS version, and app version to help diagnose issues
  • User Information: Your email address and display name to follow up on reported issues
  • Report Metadata: Date and time of report submission, issue status updates, and developer notes
  • Frequency Limit: Users can submit one issue report per week to ensure quality support

Privacy Note: Issue reports are private and only visible to you and the development team. They are never shared with other users or third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the Spendia service
  • User authentication and account management
  • Managing subscription status and premium features access
  • Processing voice and photo inputs using artificial intelligence
  • Categorizing Apple Pay transactions automatically (when enabled by you)
  • Tracking and managing monthly budgets and spending alerts
  • Enforcing daily AI usage limits (20 for free users, 100 for premium users)
  • Generating expense analytics and summaries
  • Sending push notifications for budget alerts and daily reminders (with your permission)
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Troubleshooting and fixing reported issues
  • Communicating with users about their reported issues
  • Preventing fraud and unauthorized access to our services

4. Data Storage

4.1 Cloud Storage (Firestore)

The following data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore with encryption:

  • Email address and user profile
  • Account creation date
  • Premium status (isPremium: true/false)
  • Subscription details (type, status, granted by)
  • Expense and income records
  • Monthly budgets (limits, categories, alert thresholds, current spending)
  • Custom categories
  • AI usage tracking (daily request counts per user)
  • Issue reports (title, description, steps, screen, device info, status, timestamps)

4.2 Local Device Storage

The following data is cached locally on your device:

  • Recent expenses (last 6 months)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Currency preferences

4.3 Launch User Benefits

All users who sign up during the launch period receive:

  • Free premium access to all features
  • Status marked as "launch_user" in our system
  • Continued free access even when premium subscriptions are introduced
  • Higher AI usage limits (100 requests/day instead of 20)

5. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

5.1 Firebase (Google)

We use Google Firebase for:

  • Authentication: Secure user login and account management
  • Cloud Firestore: Encrypted storage of your user data, expenses, budgets, income records, and issue reports
  • Cloud Functions: Server-side processing of AI features, budget calculations, and issue report notifications
  • App Check: Verifying requests come from legitimate instances of the Spendia app to prevent abuse
  • Remote Config: Managing feature flags and usage limits dynamically

Firebase Privacy Policy

5.2 OpenAI

We use OpenAI's API for:

  • Voice-to-text transcription and expense data extraction (GPT-3.5 Turbo)
  • Receipt photo analysis using GPT-4 Vision
  • Intelligent merchant categorization for Apple Pay transactions
  • Predicting expense categories based on descriptions

Important: Audio recordings, receipt photos, and merchant names are sent to OpenAI for processing and are NOT stored by OpenAI or Spendia after processing completes. We track usage counts to manage costs, but do not store the content you process.

OpenAI Privacy Policy

5.3 Apple Services

  • In-App Purchases: When premium subscriptions are introduced, payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not store your payment information.
  • iOS Shortcuts: Optional Apple Pay automation runs locally on your device. Transaction data is only sent to Spendia if you configure the automation.
  • DeviceCheck: Used with Firebase App Check to verify your device is legitimate without identifying you personally.

Apple's privacy policy applies to all payment and device data.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure cloud storage with Firebase encryption at rest
  • API keys stored securely in cloud infrastructure (not in app code)
  • User authentication via Firebase Auth with secure token management
  • Local device encryption for cached data
  • Firebase App Check to prevent unauthorized API access
  • Firestore Security Rules ensuring users can only access their own data
  • Access controls ensuring issue reports are only visible to the reporter and development team
  • Daily AI usage limits to prevent abuse and manage costs

7. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Subscription Information: Retained for the lifetime of your account
  • Expenses & Budgets: Retained until you delete them or your account
  • AI Usage Tracking: Daily counts retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Audio Recordings: Deleted immediately after transcription (never stored)
  • Receipt Photos: Deleted immediately after data extraction (never stored)
  • Cached Data: Limited to last 6 months, older data automatically removed from device
  • Issue Reports: Retained for the lifetime of your account to maintain a history of technical support. Deleted when you delete your account.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data (including budgets and issue reports)
  • Export your expense data (coming soon)
  • Opt-out of push notifications
  • Disable Apple Pay automation (by removing the iOS Shortcut)
  • View your current AI usage and limits
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • View your subscription status and history
  • View all your submitted issue reports and their status

9. Children's Privacy

Spendia is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Costa Rica, including the United States (where Firebase and OpenAI servers are located). These transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards including:

  • Google's and OpenAI's compliance with international data protection frameworks
  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Contractual data protection obligations

11. AI Usage Limits & Fair Use

To maintain service quality and manage costs:

  • Free Users: 20 AI requests per day (voice, photo, Apple Pay categorization combined)
  • Premium Users: 100 AI requests per day
  • Reset: Limits reset daily at midnight UTC
  • Enforcement: When you reach your daily limit, AI features will be unavailable until the next day
  • Manual Entry: Always available regardless of AI limits

12. Future Premium Features

When premium subscriptions are introduced:

  • Launch users will maintain free premium access and higher AI limits
  • New users will have the option to subscribe for premium features
  • Subscription management will be handled through Apple's App Store
  • This Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect any new data collection related to subscriptions

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending an in-app notification for material changes
  • Posting a notice in the app

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open Spendia app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to bottom
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion

All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days, including:

  • Account information and subscription status
  • All expense and income records
  • Budgets and custom categories
  • AI usage history
  • Issue reports and support history
